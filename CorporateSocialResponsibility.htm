MAKING A DIFFERENCE IN BANGLADESH

SANDOZ gives generously to the communities where employees live and work, and we view investment in local communities as an essential part of our corporate responsibility. We focus our charitable donations in two areas: health and wellness and education, with a particular focus in science education.

We believe that in order to thrive, communities need access to basic human services, such as food, clothing, and affordable healthcare. To that end, we’ve committed ourselves to partnering with organizations that meet these critical needs.

We strive to help patients, particularly the most at risk, by focusing our support in the following key areas:

• Volunteers in Medicine Clinics that provide medical treatment at no cost to economically disadvantaged individuals.

• Community organizations that provide direct service to patients and their caregivers and are based in geographic areas where we have a significant business presence. These organizations offer volunteer opportunities for SANDOZ employees in those local areas.

• Disease and therapeutically aligned organizations that provide support to patients and their caregivers and are focused in areas where we have commercial activity.

• Science &Technology to educate young people and encourage them to pursue careers in the healthcare space to meet the increasing demand for trained healthcare professionals.

• SANDOZ also continues to provide product to patients in need around the world through our Product Donation Programs.


Specialized medicines that give healthcare providers treatment choices

Our specialty medicines will meet the highest standards and span a wide spectrum of therapeutic areas. Central nervous system disorders.Oncology.Respiratory, Women’s health. We have an exciting and innovative portfolio of specialty medicines.

And we have a commitment of constantly progressing. We’re not afraid to seek new areas of unmet medical needs or challenge ourselves with hard-to-treat medical conditions.


Quality medicines you can trust

SANDOZ understands the importance of making safe and quality medicines affordable and accessible.

SANDOZ will offer generic prescription medicines which are Bangladesh DA approved and will meet the highest standards of quality and safety. We will aggressively pursue research and development efforts, making medicines more affordable and accessible to patients who need them.
